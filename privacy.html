Scout Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

About Scout

Scout is an internal desktop application developed by 1st Phorm for our social media team. Scout aggregates performance metrics from our company's official social media accounts into a unified analytics dashboard.

Important: Scout is an internal business tool used only by authorized 1st Phorm employees. It is not available to the general public.

Data We Collect

When a 1st Phorm social media account is connected to Scout, we collect the following data from supported platforms (including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, and LinkedIn):

Account Information:

  • Username and display name
  • Profile picture
  • Bio/description
  • Follower and following counts
  • Account verification status

Content Data:

  • Video and post metadata (titles, descriptions, thumbnails)
  • Post dates and durations
  • Content URLs and permalinks

Performance Metrics:

  • Views and impressions
  • Likes, comments, shares, and saves
  • Engagement rates
  • Follower growth over time

How We Use This Data

Data collected by Scout is used exclusively for:

  • Internal performance tracking and analytics
  • Generating reports for the 1st Phorm social media team
  • Identifying content trends and optimizing our social strategy
  • Comparing performance across platforms

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in our database infrastructure hosted by Supabase, which provides:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Regular security audits

Access to Scout and its data is restricted to authorized 1st Phorm team members only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

Data collected through Scout is used solely for internal business purposes. We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, or any external organizations.

Data Retention

We retain historical metrics data to enable trend analysis and year-over-year comparisons. Account connection tokens are stored securely and refreshed automatically to maintain access.

Your Rights

Since Scout only accesses 1st Phorm's own company accounts, the data belongs to 1st Phorm. If you have questions about our data practices, please contact us.

Third-Party Platforms

Scout connects to social media platforms via their official APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube/Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Instagram/Facebook) Privacy Policy
  • Pinterest Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our practices evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
